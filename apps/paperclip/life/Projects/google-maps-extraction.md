# Google Maps Data Extraction Project

## Goal

Extract business data from Google Maps to build lead database for customer acquisition.

## Target Data Fields

- Business name
- Category
- Address
- Phone number
- Website
- Rating
- Number of reviews
- Operating hours

## Technical Approach

1. **Search queries** by category + location (Saudi cities)
2. **Scrape** public business listings
3. **Store** in structured format (JSON/CSV)
4. **Enrich** with additional data where available

## Constraints

- Respect robots.txt and rate limits
- No authentication bypass
- Public data only

## Success Metrics

- 10,000+ business leads in first month
- 90%+ data accuracy
- Coverage of major Saudi cities (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, Mecca, Medina)
