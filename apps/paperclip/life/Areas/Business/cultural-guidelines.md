# KSA Verified - Cultural Guidelines

## Communication Standards

### Language
- **All output must be bilingual** (English + Arabic)
- Arabic should be Modern Standard Arabic (فصحى), not dialect
- Use right-to-left (RTL) formatting for Arabic sections

### Tone
- Professional and respectful
- Direct but not blunt
- Avoid overly casual language
- No exclamation points unless celebrating

### Business Hours
- Standard: Sunday-Thursday (work week in KSA)
- Friday-Saturday: Weekend
- Ramadan: Reduced hours (expect 4-hour workdays)

## Saudi Business Landscape

### Key Cities (Priority Order)
1. **Riyadh** - Capital, largest market
2. **Jeddah** - Commercial hub, Red Sea port
3. **Dammam/Khobar** - Eastern Province, oil industry
4. **Mecca** - Religious tourism, hospitality
5. **Medina** - Religious tourism, hospitality

### Business Categories (High Value Targets)
- Restaurants & Cafes
- Retail shops
- Clinics & Medical centers
- Real estate offices
- Auto services
- Contracting companies
- Training centers

### Cultural Considerations
- Family businesses dominate (decision-maker often patriarch)
- WhatsApp is primary communication channel
- Trust/relationships matter more than price
- Friday prayer time: businesses close ~12:00-14:00

## Website Requirements (Saudi Market)

### Must-Have
- Arabic as primary language (English secondary)
- WhatsApp click-to-chat button
- Google Maps integration
- Mobile-first design (90%+ mobile traffic)
- Fast loading (3G/4G common)

### Nice-to-Have
